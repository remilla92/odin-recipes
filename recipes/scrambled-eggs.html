Scrambled eggs are a great part of any meal, filling and nutrious, they are a good use for excess eggs and a quick meal to make.

To make scrambled eggs you will need the following:

  • 2 Eggs (Large)
  • 1 Medium pan
  • 2 table spoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of chedar Cheese

To make scrambled eggs is quite easy simply follow the below steps

  1. Heat a pan to medium.
  2. Then you crack 2 eggs into the pan
  3. Whisk the eggs to quickly scramble them
  4. Add butter and Cheese to flavor the eggs
  5. Serve the eggs
