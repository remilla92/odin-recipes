Baked Potato

Baked potatos are simply the best, nothing better even in a stew. Here is a quick guide to make your own.

Directions

First you will need a few items:

  • Your spud
  • Netural Oil, like canola or vegetable
  • Salt
  • Toppings, (butter, sour cream, cheese, bacon)

To make the baked potato follow the below steps

  1. Wash your spud
  2. Rub your spud in oil
  3. Lightly coat your spud in salt
  4. Place your spud in the oven at 375Â° F for 2 hours
  5. Once your spud is fork tender all the way through, take it out of the oven
  6. Cut your spud open and serve. Add as many or as few toppings as you like.
