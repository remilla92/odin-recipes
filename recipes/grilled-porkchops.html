Making grilled porkchops is a delecious and quick way to get a meal ready.

To make grilled porkchops you will need the following:

  • 1 8oz Porkchop
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • A grill

To grill your chops, just follow the easy steps below:

  1. Start your charcoal about 30 minutes before you ready to grill, or light your gas gril about 5 minutes before
  2. Lightly season your porkchops on both sides with salt and pepper
  3. Place your porkchops on the grill, flipping about every 2 minutes.
  4. Continue cooking until porkchops are fully cooked, 145 degrees internal
  5. Remove porkchops and let rest, after 5 minutes you may serve
